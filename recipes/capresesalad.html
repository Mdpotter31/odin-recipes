Caprese Salad

Caprese Salad is an easy, classic Italian salad.

Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe tomatoes
  • 8 oz (225g) fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze (or balsamic vinegar)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Slice the tomatoes and fresh mozzarella into 1/4-inch thick slices.
  2. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving platter, alternating between them.
  3. Place a fresh basil leaf on top of each tomato and mozzarella slice.
  4. Drizzle the extra virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze (or vinegar) over the salad.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Serve immediately and enjoy!
