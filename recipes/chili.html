Chili Mac

This hearty and flavorful dish combines the best of chili and macaroni into one satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 88 oz (225g) elbow macaroni
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced (optional)
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1Shredded cheese, chopped green onions, or sour cream for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the elbow macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet or pot, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat until browned and cooked through. Break it up into crumbles as it cooks.
  3. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced bell pepper (if using) to the skillet with the ground beef. Saut√© for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are softened.
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes (with their juices) and the rinsed and drained kidney beans. Add the chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  5. Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the chili mixture simmer for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. If the mixture becomes too thick, you can add a little water or beef broth to reach your desired consistency.
  6. Add the cooked elbow macaroni to the skillet or pot with the chili mixture. Stir everything together until well combined and heated through.
  7. Serve the Chili Mac hot in bowls. Optionally, top each serving with shredded cheese, chopped green onions, or a dollop of sour cream for added flavor and creaminess.

This Quick and Easy Chili Mac recipe is sure to become a family favorite!
