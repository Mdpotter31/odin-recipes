Chicken Alfredo

This Chicken Alfredo recipe is designed to save you time in the kitchen without compromising on taste. In just over 30 minutes, you'll have a mouthwatering meal ready to be enjoyed.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) fettuccine pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 lb (450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), thinly sliced or diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned, about 5-6 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and the bell peppers to the pot and saut√© for an additional 1 minute.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened, about 3-4 minutes.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine pasta to the pot and toss until well coated in the sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped fresh parsley, if desired.

Serve the chicken Alfredo pasta immediately and enjoy!
